### Low level

Metadata is split into two parts: the collection stores collection‑wide data, and the item stores item‑specific data (**not full metadata**). To obtain full metadata:

1. Resolve the NFT item address by index from the collection (if needed ) with `get_nft_address_by_index(index)`.
1. Get item individual content from the item contract with `get_nft_data()`.
1. Get full metadata from the collection contract with `get_nft_content(index, individual_content)`.
