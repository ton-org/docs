## Requirements

- iOS 13.0+
- Swift 5.9+

## Adding WalletKit to XCode project

1. Open your project in XCode
1. Go to `File` â†’ `Add Package Dependencies`
1. Search for WalletKit package by URL: [`https://github.com/ton-connect/kit-ios.git`](https://github.com/ton-connect/kit-ios.git)
1. Tap the <kbd>Add Package</kbd> button

## Adding WalletKit to your Swift Package

1. Open your package's `Package.swift` manifest file
1. Add dependency:

   ```swift
   dependencies: [
       .package(url: "https://github.com/ton-connect/kit-ios.git", .upToNextMajor(from: "0.0.2"))
   ]
   ```

1. Add product to your target:

   ```swift
   .product(name: "TONWalletKit", package: "TONWalletKit")
   ```
