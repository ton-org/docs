[TON Builders](https://builders.ton.org) helps you manage your SDK keys and participate in various support programs from TON Foundation.

Register your project and go to the **Analytics** tab.

<Image
  src="/resources/images/tma-analytics/analytics-preparations-1.png"
  darkSrc="/resources/images/tma-analytics/analytics-preparations-1.png"
  alt="Analytics tab in TON Builders"
/>

Enter your Telegram Bot URL and mini app domain to receive an **API key** for SDK initialization. You can also manage your existing keys from the same section.
